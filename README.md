## 🧬 Research & Projects

### Head & Neck Cancer Tumouroid — Differential Expression Pipeline
An end-to-end R-based pipeline for differential gene expression analysis in patient-derived head and neck cancer tumouroids. Contributed bioinformatics analysis to a PhD research project in cancer biology, producing a validated 5-marker panel (**MMP1, MMP13, MMP11, CA9, MAGEA4**) for downstream wet lab validation via immunofluorescence.
- Tools: `DESeq2` · `R` · `ggplot2` · `RMarkdown`

### MICR3004 — Metagenomic Analysis of Australian Mine Leachate Microbiomes
Shotgun metagenomic sequencing study profiling the taxonomic and functional diversity of microbial communities in bioleaching columns from two Australian sulfidic mine sites (Mount Garnet, QLD & Princess Creek, TAS). Identified *Acidithiobacillus ferrooxidans* as the dominant bioleaching taxon and characterised site-specific KEGG metabolic pathways driving iron/sulphur oxidation and AMD resistance.
- Tools: `MetaPhlAn3` · `HUMAnN3` · `MetaBAT2` · `FastQC` · `R`
- Methods: shotgun sequencing · MAG reconstruction · PCA · KEGG pathway analysis · taxonomic profiling

### DECO7180 — Canopy (Biophilic Micro-Recovery App)
UX research and prototype development for *Canopy*, a biophilic micro-recovery app designed for night-shift nurses experiencing circadian disruption. Built across multiple sprint phases with user research, card-sort probes, and interactive HTML/CSS/JS prototypes.
- Team: UX Shoreline (UQ DECO7180)
- [View repository →](https://github.com/k-bhardwajj/DECO7180-UX-Shoreline-)
